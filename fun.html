Why do people enjoy Pac-Man?

There is no doubt that Pac-Man has changed video gaming forever. Many videogame companys base thier games on the way Pac-Man is programmed.

PacMan is fun because unlike other video games, the goal of this game is not to shoot/destroy the other objects/opponents but trying to stay clear of their way.

Many people find this interesting because although many games involve violance, this game does not include. This is also a game of strategy and focus which helps strengthen the brain and improve muscle memory.

There are also many games simialr to Pac-Man that people who play it would enjoy such as minesweeper, breakout, tetris, slither.io, mario bros and many more.
